Merge Blocks Neon Chain – Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-26

Overview

This privacy policy explains how Merge Blocks Neon Chain (“the App”) handles information.

Information we collect

  • No account data: The App does not require sign-up and does not collect your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Local game data: The App stores gameplay progress and settings locally on your device (e.g., using shared preferences).

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad delivery, frequency capping, fraud prevention, and analytics, in accordance with Google’s policies.

In-app purchases (Google Play Billing)

The App offers in-app purchases. Purchase processing is handled by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Children’s privacy

The App is a casual puzzle game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Advertising and purchase flows (if used) are provided through Google’s systems and governed by their policies.
